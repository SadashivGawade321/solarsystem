Textures in this pack are based on NASA elevation and imagery data. Colors and shades of the textures are tuned according to true-color photos made by Messenger, Viking, and Cassini spacecrafts, and, of course, the Hubble Space Telescope.

NOTES:

  • Some parts of the planets remain to be mapped. These “gaps” are filled with fictional terrain that corresponds with the rest of the landscape.
  • The colors are slightly more saturated to highlight a unique nature for each object.
  • Earth textures are the most precise part of the pack due to merging and adjusting large amounts of geo-data, space photos, and NASA’s Blue Marble images.
  • True Earth colors may vary depending on camera, exposure, lighting, and atmospheric conditions.
  • Seasonal variations affect Earth’s flora and snow coverage, with more vegetation and less snow.
